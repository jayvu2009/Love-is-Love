About Us

Love is Love is an interactive storytelling project dedicated to honoring LGBTQ+ relationships and identities across cultures. Our goal is to create a respectful digital space where stories foster empathy, challenge stigma, and invite audiences to see love beyond boundaries.

Through thoughtful design and personal narratives, we aim to make awareness tangible, human, and memorable for everyone who visits.

Emotional Storytelling

Honest stories that connect people through compassion, dignity, and lived experience.

Interactive Design

Accessible and engaging web interaction that guides visitors with clarity and care.

Promoting Inclusivity

A platform that encourages allyship, understanding, and collective respect.
